Big10 Softball Dashboard

Using play-by-play data from the NCAA website, a dashboard including information for all Big10 teams is being developed. The dashboard includes spray chart, counts, and a variety of statistic measures. The dashboard is being created to provide support to the University of Michigan coaching staff for game and practice decisions.

In order to develop this project I used got data from API, used databases, and created visualizations with Matpltlib and Plotly.

Spotify Price vs. Follower

This project took an analytical approach to Spotify Discover Weekly recommendations and see if there are any underlying correlations between the number of followers and average track price of the top ten tracks pers artist. This project used the Spotify, iTunes, and Taste Dive API.

University Health Service

I manage five research projects including tracking on campus Covid-19 testing and the usage of medical and quick clinics which has revealed students were not using virtual options, so staffing was shifted to offer more in person appointments. I provide diverse administrative support to the Quality improvement chair by tracking and modeling hand sanitation improving rates to a consistent 90% for all clinical staff members.

Farmers Insurance

I developed an Insurance Agent Assessment Tool as a part of the Research and Development Team that will be used by five departments, totaling over 200 employees across the company through Farmers Insurance internal online toolbox. I analyzed data from 40,000 agents in Alteryx and worked in partnership with product managers to study success rates of agents and discovered that the bottom 5% of agents contributed to over 10% of losses in the company.
